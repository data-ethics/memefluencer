Why never search on Google?


Google has become such huge name in Online search, that we now use term "googling" for searching online. With larger number of users, and it's huge machine learning models Google is now a big fish in the sea of targated Ads world. Looking at Targated ad surfacially, looks great, because advertiser get their potential customers and users get only relevent ads. If story had ended here, would have been great story, but targated ad can be used for intentional manipualtion of users and can be threat to democracy as well. Targated ad manipulation got huge attention, with Cambridge Analytica Scandel in 2018, as the wistle blower Christopher Wylie, interviewed with The gardian and the New York times. If you wish to know more about this scandel you can watch Netflix Documentry "The great Hack"

With every use, google stores users activities online and add to the users profile. This profile is used for personalized search results and personalized targated ads. Due to profiling and personalized search result, users are only exposed to the information that has been filtered to fit their need, and this effect is well known as “filter bubble”. In this way google and YouTube search results only shows the results that the search engines thinks the user wants to see. This limitation of information with algorithmic filtering causes a user to be less likely to find information that contradicts their profile. This also limits a users to know a different views on that topic. Research has shown that by displaying search results that are favorable to one candidate, the search engine manipulation effect can even affect the results of elections. If this is done with intention to change the election results in favoure of one candidate , it will be highly unethical practice and is threat to democracy. This is what cambridge analytics and Trump's election campaign, is accused of.

How a person's profile can be build and , what google search queries let's us know about person's life and psychology has been beautifully shown in the interactive documentry "Made to Measure" . Here you can interactivly participate along side the documentry and also know about yourself with little data you provide during the watch time.

How to live without Google?

  • Change the default Browser and default search engine:
    • There are some alternative to Google Chrome and Google search engine that protects your privacy and doesn't tareget you with targated ads. For example you can use DuckduckGo instead of Google seach and set this as default search engine. Download and use Firefox or TOR as default and secure Browsers. To change the default search engine:
    • Go to setting of the browser
    • Select Search on left bar
    • Select Standard Search Engine
    • Select DuckDuckGo from the dropdown option
  • Move your Passwords and Bookmarks to new Browser:
    • After using a browser for a long time changing to a new browser could be problematic in terms of moving your book marks you saved, and also the passwords. It is wise to save your user name and password to some external drive than to save it in browsers, but you can easily move your passwords and Bookmarks from Chrome to Fire fox. When you install Firefox you get an default options to import your passwords and bookmarks from Chrome. It is smooth and easy.
  • Turn off personalized ads
    • Go to your Google Account.
    • In the navigation panel, select Data & privacy.
    • Scroll to "Things you've done and places you've been."
    • Under "Ad settings," select Ad personalization.
    • Turn off the switch next to "Ad Personalization is ON."

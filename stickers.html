CogSci Signal sticker contest will run from October 20 until October 30, 2022. Sticker submissions are accepted through this form. Voting for the best sticker will open on October 31 and run until November 5. The winner will be announced on November 6. You are encouraged to share your sticker (and its backstory, if it has one) in the CogSci Signal group (ask a fellow CogSci student for the invitation link).

Contest rules

  • Create a new CogSci-themed sticker pack for Signal (a sticker pack includes at minimum one sticker)
  • It is a good idea to follow the official Signal sticker creator guidelines (these guidelines also include a step-by-step tutorial on how to create stickers)
  • Stickers should not contain any offensive content related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, religion, or sexual imagery
  • Submit your sticker pack by 23:59 on October 30, 2022 using this form
  • Share your sticker in the CogSci group (optional)
  • Between October 31 and November 5, vote for your favourite sticker submission
  • The winner will get a copy of Weapons of Math Destruction by Cathy O'Neil

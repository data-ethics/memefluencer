Memefluencer is a Data Ethics Outreach Lab project. As part of our study project, we prepare outreach materials whose aim is to make more people aware of the extent of user data collection Google does through its search engine and the Chrome browser. Furthermore, we aim to inspire more people to opt for privacy-focused messaging apps such as Signal.

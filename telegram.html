CogSci Signal Sticker Contest

CogSci Signal sticker contest will run from October 20 to October 30, 2022. Sticker submissions are accepted through the CogSci group on Signal (ask a fellow CogSci student for the invitation link) and this form. Voting for the best sticker will open on October 31 and run until November 7. The winner will be announced on November 8.

Contest rules

  • Create a new CogSci-themed sticker pack for Signal (a sticker pack includes at minimum one sticker)
  • It is a good idea to follow the official Signal sticker creator guidelines
  • Submit your sticker pack by 23:59 on October 30, 2022
  • Between October 31 and November 7, vote for your favourite sticker submission
